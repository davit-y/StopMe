Stop Me

Thanks for installing Stop Me!

To get started visit a site click the StopMe icon in the extensions section at the top right of your browser to create a daily alarm.

Once an alarm is created you will be able to view your usage of that site, and edit, disable, and delete the alarm.

The alarms section below shows active alarms and allows you to set the time that daily usage is reset back to 0 for all sites.
